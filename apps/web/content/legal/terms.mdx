## 2. Description of Service

Anarlog is an open-source note-taking and meeting transcription application. It helps you capture, organize, and manage your notes and meeting context.

### 2.1 Local-First Architecture

Anarlog is designed as a local-first application. Your notes, transcripts, and meeting data are stored locally on your device in plain markdown files. You can run Anarlog completely offline using a local language model (e.g., via LM Studio or Ollama). Cloud-based features — such as cloud transcription and AI summarization — are optional and require an internet connection.

### 2.2 Account and Cloud Features
